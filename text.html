blogs on my town

November, 2021

The place where i used live is my town Name wadi junction. its come in dist of kalaburgi State Karnataka. In my town there is huge ACC cement plant in which half of the population of my town used to work there.Other people have there own business like stone mines. Stone mines are huge market in my town.

Related to the stone business my family business is also the same my father have a business of stone polishing in which stone will polished and delivered.In my town there is huge possibility for the business line but there is no opportunity for good jobs and employment for the educated person, because in my town there is no awareness for the encouragment of the education. I saw many children who were not aware of educations they used to do work instead studing in school they used work in the stone mines .

Apart from my town ,talking about my family. My family have six member my father,my mother,my elder brother and my sister, my father is a business man, my mother is a house wife, my big brother is a employee ACC cement company in madhya pradesh and my second big brother he is in gulf country Qatar,he is poreman in oil and refinery company
